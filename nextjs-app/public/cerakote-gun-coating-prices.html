Cerakote Gun Coating Prices

Basic Pricing

General Information

We prefer to receive items Fully assembled or Fully disassembled (just the parts that are being coated and Please do not include xtra parts in the packaging). If you send us a disassembled gun or gun parts, you must include an itemized inventory/packing list. We will not be responsible for lost parts (Be Sure To Use Our Work Request Form) if no inventory/packing list is included. Re-Finishing your firearm with Cerakote or DuraCoat does not include the removal of existing pitting, dings or scratches (except minor imperfections removed with normal blasting/etching procedure). Limited metal preparation is available at additional cost on a per-hour basis. We can provide a quote once we have received your items. Please note that our prices do not include sales tax (if applicable), return shipping, handling or insurance charges. This price list below does not list all of our services. Please contact us via email if you have any questions. (We are not able to staff the phone without significant price increases) If you need information on a service not listed here or if you have any questions regarding our pricing and services please EMAIL us (for fastest response)and we will get back to you right away.

Shipping

We recommend shipping via Usps, UPS or FedEx - Handguns Fed ex/Ups (not required to be shipped overnight per Atf), long arms (Usps seems best value). Pack your firearm or parts securely, and in packaging that is solid enough for us to return your newly reconditioned item to you using the same packaging. Include your name, return shipping address (no PO Boxes, per ATF), daytime telephone number and email address. We will send a copy of your order confirmation and an estimated delivery date with pricing (excluding return shipping and insurance). Please read our complete shipping instructions in the FAQ section , where you will find complete instructions on using commercial carriers.

Basic Pricing

This is our base price list. Simply go down the list adding up the items you desire to be done for a grand total.  Since we provide custom services it is impossible to create a one size fits all price list. We will provide you with a detailed estimate if you fill out our quote request form. This allows us to get a clearer picture of your exact needs as well as provide you with a written estimate. Our goal is to insure you get the highest quality workmanship delivered in a timely manner, while striving to provide prices that are very competitive.
