BARREL THREADING SERVICES & THREAD PROTECTORS

Barrels are dialed in as close to .0001  as possible at two points. I use a long stem Interapid indicator to insure that the last 2.700 is running as strait as possible. I feel this is very important to make sure that the bullet travels strait down the suppressor with no baffle strikes. Thread Protectors may be blended in with the barrel for a seamless finish or knurled to your preference.
