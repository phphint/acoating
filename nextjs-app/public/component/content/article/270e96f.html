Limited Lifetime Warranty

Alternative Firearm Coatings metal finishes carry a limited lifetime warranty against finish failure. Finish failures that are covered include corrosion through the finish and peeling or flaking of the finish.  Conditions that will not be covered are failure of the finish resulting from misuse, abuse, accident, improper cleaning or modifications performed after the metal finish has been applied.
