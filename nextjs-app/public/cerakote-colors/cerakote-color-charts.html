Cerakote Solid Colors (H-Series Charts)

Shown below are the standard Cerakote solid colors available. You can combine different colors on the same gun. Take a look at our Gun Finish Simulator to get an idea of how your color choices might look like together. You are not limited to the colors shown below, the possible color combination's are limited only by your imagination.

 

Please Note:  What finish to use depends on the project. Cerakote H-Series (baked on) and has the highest wear test results of all coatings compared. Cerakote is available in C-SERIES (air dry) for use on Plastics, Wood, Scopes etc. C-Series Cerakote wear test are similar to Duracoat on the test result page.
